Artisan Crafts

E-commerce Website

Project Overview

Artisan Crafts needed an e-commerce platform to showcase and sell their handmade products. We created a visually appealing, user-friendly online store that highlights the craftsmanship of their items while providing a seamless shopping experience.

Challenge

The main challenge was to create a website that would showcase the unique, handcrafted nature of the products while providing all the functionality of a modern e-commerce platform. The client also needed a simple way to manage inventory and process orders.

Solution

We developed a custom e-commerce website with high-quality product photography, detailed product descriptions, and an intuitive shopping experience. The site includes advanced filtering options, a secure checkout process, and a robust inventory management system.

Client

Artisan Crafts Co.

Timeline

8 weeks

Services

E-commerce Development, UI/UX Design, Product Photography, SEO

Technologies

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, WooCommerce, WordPress

Results

  • 43% increase in online sales
  • 65% increase in average time on site
  • 28% reduction in cart abandonment

Creative Studio

Portfolio Website

Project Overview

Creative Studio needed a portfolio website to showcase their graphic design and branding work. We created a visually stunning, interactive portfolio that highlights their creative projects and attracts potential clients.

Challenge

The main challenge was to design a website that would reflect the client's creative expertise while ensuring that their work remained the focal point. The site needed to be visually impressive without overshadowing the portfolio pieces.

Solution

We developed a minimalist yet impactful portfolio website with smooth animations, a filterable project gallery, and detailed project case studies. The design emphasizes the client's work through large imagery and clean typography, creating an immersive browsing experience.

Client

Creative Studio Inc.

Timeline

6 weeks

Services

Web Design, Development, Animation, Content Strategy

Technologies

HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, GSAP, Intersection Observer

Results

  • Featured in 3 design publications
  • 35% increase in social media followers
